js-whatever/js/duration-format

require("js-whatever/js/duration-format")(ms, strings, precision) → {string}

Rounds a duration to the specified precision. The duration is represented in terms of the largest possible interval (from days, hours, minutes, seconds, milliseconds)

Parameters:

The duration to format in milliseconds
